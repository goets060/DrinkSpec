The Science Behind DrinkSpec

Utilization of Micro-Electro-Mechanical-Systems (MEMS) Mass Spectrometry

The Sentinel utilizes cutting-edge MEMS technology to provide laboratory-grade analysis in a portable, robust form factor. Below are the core modules that power our detection capabilities.

Ionization Source

  • High-Efficiency Penning Trap: Utilizes a powerful neodymium magnetic field and triple-silicon electrode configuration to maximize ionization yield, ensuring reliable operation even in ultra-high vacuum conditions.
  • Adaptive Voltage Control: Automatically adjusts between 500V and 1500V based on pressure levels to maintain a consistent and precise ion beam.
  • Optimized Energy Output: Engineered to produce a stable ion beam with peak intensity at 15 eV, providing the ideal foundation for accurate chemical identification.

Mass Analyzer

  • Monolithic Silicon-Glass Design: Features a high-precision Quadrupole Analyzer etched from silicon layers and glass spacers, resulting in a durable, "sandwich-style" monolithic device.
  • Superior Geometry: Despite its miniature scale, the internal electrode architecture mimics optimal hyperbolic geometries to ensure high-resolution filtering of chemical ions.
  • Precision Miniaturization: At a total length of 70mm, the analyzer strikes the perfect balance between professional-grade resolution and compact, portable dimensions.

Detector

  • Dual-Layer Faraday Cup: Features a specialized silicon-glass aperture design that precisely captures and measures ions for immediate data processing.
  • Modular Sensitivity: The architecture supports an optional Microchannel Plate (MCP) electron multiplier, allowing for significantly enhanced detection sensitivity when required.
  • High Flexibility: Designed for adaptable configurations, ensuring the Sentinel can be tuned for various operational environments without hardware overhauls.

3D Printed Housing

  • Aerospace-Grade Durability: The housing is engineered to withstand harsh conditions—including the vibrations of a rocket launch—ensuring the analysis modules remain perfectly aligned.
  • High-Precision Prototyping: Fabricated using advanced resin-based 3D printing with resolutions finer than 50 micrometers for perfect component integration and electrical insulation.
  • Optimized Gas Flow: The design features specialized intake apertures that allow ambient gases to enter the ionization chamber efficiently while maintaining the structural integrity of the vacuum environment.

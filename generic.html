All About the DrinkSpec "Sentinel"

Every year, millions of people find themselves in situations where they question the safety of their drink. Currently, our only defense is ‘watch your drink’ or unreliable, paper-based test strips that are prone to errors and leave us guessing. We don't rely on guesswork for anything else in our digital lives—why do we rely on it for our personal safety? Introducing the DrinkSpec Sentinel. It’s the world’s first handheld, lab-grade chemical screener. It pairs with your phone to tell you exactly what’s in your glass in under 30 seconds. With the Sentinel, you aren't just drinking; you’re staying informed. We aren't just selling a device; we’re selling the freedom to enjoy your night out with data-driven confidence. Because safety isn't a feeling, it’s a fact.
